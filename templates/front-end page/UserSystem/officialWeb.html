Models

Stock Prediction Machines

SVM

(Support Vector Machine)

The SVM model provides robust and precise stock
predictions by finding the optimal hyperplane that
separates different market trends. Best suited for
those who prefer a deterministic approach to
forecasting.

GRU

(Gated Recurrent Unit)

Our GRU model offers efficient and accurate stock
price predictions by capturing long-term
dependencies in sequential data. Ideal for those
looking for fast and reliable results with lower
computational costs.

LSTM

(Long Short-Term Memory)

The LSTM model excels in predicting stock market
trends by effectively managing long-term
dependencies and short-term patterns. Perfect for
users seeking high accuracy in volatile market
conditions.
